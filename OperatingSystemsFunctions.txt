MacOS has great built in gesture support. For instance, flicking upward with three fingers puts the current window away. Flicking to the right makes a webpage go backwards. Force touch activates the haptic feedback as well as quick look.
The finder is a great tool for file organziation. Clicking into the finder opens a window which displays your files, It works in a tab display so it's easy to keep track of the heirachy of files.
MacOS is well integrated with it's mobile counterparts. It's easy to view notifications you get on your Mac and vise-versa. It's also easy to copy one thing on a mac or iphone and paste it into another device.
